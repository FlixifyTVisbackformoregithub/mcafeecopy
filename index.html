Rebooting Your Device When It's in Danger

If your device is acting erratically or is at risk, here are some methods to safely reboot:

  • Windows: Click on the Start menu, select 'Power', and choose 'Restart'. If unresponsive, hold the power button for 10 seconds until it turns off, then press it again to turn it back on.
  • Mac: Click on the Apple logo in the top left corner, and select 'Restart'. For unresponsive systems, press and hold the power button until it shuts down, then press it again to reboot.
  • Mobile Devices: Typically, hold the power button until the power menu appears. Choose 'Restart'. If frozen, hold the power and volume down buttons simultaneously until it resets.
  • Tablets: Press and hold the power button to restart.
